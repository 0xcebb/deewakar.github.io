** Headlines

    Main Headline starts with *, subheadings are created by adding more asterisks.

** Show/Hide contents
   TAB will cycle local subtree, Shift-TAB cycles through states globally.

** Links to other places

*** Hyperlinks are of the format [[Link] [description]]

    e.g. [[https://orgmode.org/worg/org-tutorials/orgtutorial_dto.html][Org mode tutorial]]

    e.g. [[./other/testfile.org][anther org file]]

*** Internal links are written as [[ target ] ] and dedicated targets are marked with angular brackets <<target>>
    e.g. go to the TOP [[TOP]]

*** Other external links
    Files are linked with `file` 
    e.g. `file:./sample.txt`
Links can be visited with C-c C-o. [[https://orgmode.org/manual/External-Links.html#External-Links][external links documentation]]



** Ordered and unordered lists
*** ordered lists have numbers

1. first
2. second
3. third

*** unordered lists start with '-', '+' or '*'
- blabla
- foo
- bar

+ baz
+ fizzbuzz

** Blocks
   Blocks are enclosed inside "#+BEGIN" and "#+END"
   
#+BEGIN_SRC elisp

(defun org-xor (a b)
    "Exclusive or."
    (if a (not b) b))

#+END_SRC

The source blocks can be edited in native mode with C-c '.

** Math symbols and equations
*** subscripts with _ and superscripts with ^
- The speed of light is 3 x 10^8 m/s
- let f_{0} be the the initial function. 

Toggle WYSISYM mode with C-c C-x \

*** Latex-like symbols
- The circumference of a circle is given by 2\pi{}r
- As \alpha \to 0, \gamma \to \infin
  
Toggle WYSISYM mode with C-c C-x \

*** Latex fragments

#+HTML_MATHJAX: align: left   
\begin{align}
x&=\sqrt{b} \\
y&=x^2 \\
\end{align}

*** Inline latex 
Latex code can be embedded inline by enclosing inside '\( \)' or '\[ \]'
We're given, \(E = mc^2\) and \(T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}\)


preview latex fragments on point with C-c C-x C-l
Note: make sure you have latex (texlive) and dvipng installed
